Eveluating the Austrian NR elections using the First Past The Post

With the US election coming up this November it is interesting how the election results and the distribution of members in the lower house of the parliament and the Government of Austria would look like if we used the First Past the Post election system. It is interesting to see if the structure of the results is similar to the USA.

We especially look at some of the most popular points of critique and tried to find these points were true for Austria as well.

Interesting findings

  • Fairness
    • Largest parties receive an "unfair" "seat bonus"
    • Smaller parties are disadvantaged
  • Smocal Parties
